Harry Potter

By J.K. Rowling

Fantasy

Harry Potter discovers he's a wizard on his 11th birthday and is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Along with his new friends Ron and Hermione, Harry begins to unravel the mystery of the Sorcerer's Stone and his own past, while facing the dark wizard who killed his parents.

Alice in Wonderland

By Lewis Carroll

Fantasy

Alice follows a white rabbit down a rabbit hole into a magical wonderland. There she encounters peculiar characters like the Mad Hatter, the Cheshire Cat, and the Queen of Hearts. Through her adventures, Alice learns to question reality and embrace the absurd while growing in confidence.

The Gruffalo

By Julia Donaldson

Children's

A clever mouse takes a walk through the woods, encountering various predators. To deter them from eating him, he invents a fearsome creature called the Gruffalo. To his surprise, he eventually meets a real Gruffalo! Using his wit, the mouse manages to convince even the Gruffalo that he is the scariest creature in the woods.

Matilda

By Roald Dahl

Children's

Matilda is a brilliant young girl with negligent parents and a terrifying school principal. She discovers she has telekinetic powers and uses them to help her beloved teacher Miss Honey and stand up to injustice. Through books and learning, she finds the strength to change her destiny.

Where the Wild Things Are

By Maurice Sendak

Picture Book

When Max misbehaves and is sent to bed without supper, his room transforms into a magical forest. He sails to an island where the Wild Things live and becomes their king. After a wild rumpus, Max begins to miss home and returns to find his supper waiting - still warm.
